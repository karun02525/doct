# HMIEvo QR App

## Overview

### Business Objective

The **HMIEvo QR App** initiative aims to provide **seamless and instant access** to the Smart Source and Star Source application through QR code scanning. By simplifying the app discovery, download, and launch process, this solution reduces friction for users and significantly improves adoption and overall user experience.

Key objectives include:

* Enabling quick access to the Smart Source or Star Source app via QR codes
* Improving user adoption by eliminating manual search and navigation steps
* Enhancing user experience by intelligently redirecting users based on device and app installation status


---

## Vision & Opportunity

The vision of the HMIEvo QR App is to create a **frictionless entry point** to the Smart Source or Star Source ecosystem.

This opportunity enables:

* Instant access to the app within **1–2 seconds** of scanning a QR code
* Compatibility across all supported **iOS and Android phones and tablets**
* A visually appealing, branded landing experience featuring **Freightliner** and **Smart Source** branding
* A visually appealing, branded landing experience featuring **Western Star** and **Star Source** branding

---

## Target Use Cases

| Use Case            | Persona | Problem Statement                                      | Product Behavior                              |
| ------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------- |
| Quick App Launch    | User    | Wants immediate access if the app is already installed | Scan QR → Redirect directly to app            |
| App Download Prompt | User    | Does not have the app installed                        | Scan QR → Redirect to App Store / Google Play |
